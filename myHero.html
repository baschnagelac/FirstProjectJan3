The Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man!

The friendly neighborhood Spider-Man, otherwise known as Peter Parker, was bitten by a radioactive spider that gave him the power to be the hero of New York City. Peter Parker also works as a photographer for the Daily Bugle. Peter Parker was orphaned and raised by his Aunt and Uncle in Queens, New York where he met his friends before becoming Spider-Man. Once coming into power, he found enemies. He is best known for saving New York City using his "Spidey Senses", web slinging, super strength, and climbing walls.
